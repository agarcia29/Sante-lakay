What Is Sante Lakay

Peace of mind, delivered across the water.

Sante Lakay ("Home Health") is a medical membership built for Haitians living abroad. You enroll and pay from wherever you are — the United States, Canada, France, the Dominican Republic, anywhere. Your family in Haiti gets a direct line to emergency medical support whenever they need it, backed by a dispatch center and a network of ambulance, air, and ground rescue partners on the ground in Haiti.

Built on family, not just coverage

Haitian culture is collectivist — caring for family abroad is an honor, not an obligation. Sante Lakay gives that instinct a reliable system.

Care that speaks your family's language

Our dispatch team and Guardian Network communicate with your family in Haitian Creole, not just English or French — so nothing gets lost when it matters most.

A trusted local network

Our dispatch center and rescue partners are based in Port-au-Prince, with a growing Guardian Network of medical professionals in rural regions.

Traditional insurance reimburses medical bills after the fact. You pay first, file a claim, and wait. It doesn't answer the phone when something happens — it just pays for it later, if you're covered.

Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

Who can I enroll in a Sante Lakay membership?+

Any family member living in Haiti — parents, grandparents, siblings, children, or extended family you support. A Family plan covers up to four people, with room to add more.

How does my family in Haiti request help?+

They call one dispatch number, available 24/7. Our team verifies membership status and coordinates the appropriate response — ground ambulance, air evacuation, or hospital transfer.

Where can I enroll from?+

Anywhere. Sante Lakay is built for the Haitian diaspora worldwide — the United States, Canada, France, the Dominican Republic, and beyond.

Does this include full health insurance?+

Not yet. Today's memberships cover emergency coordination, ambulance, and evacuation services. Comprehensive health insurance plans — covering ER visits, hospitalization, and pharmacy — are launching soon.

Can I cancel or change my plan?+

Yes. You can manage your membership, update payment details, and adjust who's covered directly from your account once your member portal is active.
