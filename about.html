About Sante Lakay

Built for the way Haitian families actually take care of each other.

Sante Lakay means "Home Health." We exist so that caring for family in Haiti doesn't depend on being physically there when something goes wrong.

Our Mission

To provide and sell a medical membership — Sante Lakay, "Home Health" — to the Haitian diaspora everywhere in the world, giving their families in Haiti improved access to health care through a simple monthly or annual plan.

Why This Matters

A culture built on looking after each other.

Haiti is a country and culture built on collectivist values. Interdependence — providing for family members near and far — isn't an obligation, it's a given. Sante Lakay exists to give that instinct a reliable system to work through.

An honor, not a burden

Unlike the individualist cultures of the US and Canada, Haitians abroad see sending money, services, and support home as a privilege and an honor when given the chance to move abroad.

Security in uncertain times

Haiti regularly experiences economic and political instability. When conditions worsen, diaspora support for family back home has historically increased — Sante Lakay channels that support into reliable medical access.

Trust over transfers

Rather than trusting that a money transfer will cover a hospital bill correctly, families can rely on Sante Lakay for the full journey — from the first call to the care itself.

How We Operate

A coordinated network, not a call center alone.

Sante Lakay contracts established organizations in Haiti to fulfill every part of the membership, so members get a real medical response — not just a phone tree.

Rescue & Ambulance

HERO Client Rescue S.A.

Our ambulance and rescue partnership, providing ground transport, in-country flights, and patient advocacy for every member.

Rural Coverage

The Guardian Network

Local doctors and medical professionals stationed in areas outside Port-au-Prince, equipped with life-saving equipment and connected to local hospitals.

Our Objective

What Sante Lakay means for Haiti.

Beyond individual memberships, Sante Lakay is built to strengthen healthcare access across the country — funded by the same memberships that protect your family.

Improved access to medical care for members and, over time, their communities
Health initiatives within rural and underserved communities
Employment for local Haitian doctors, dispatchers, and rescue teams
Capacity building alongside partners like HERO and HALO, established in Haiti
Our Base Abroad

Why Sante Lakay is headquartered in South Florida.

South Florida is home to one of the largest Haitian communities in the world, and it gives Sante Lakay a stable base to support operations in Haiti — especially when things get difficult there.

01

Banking support

When banks in Haiti close during political or economic crises, our USA-based operations can keep supporting payroll, fuel, and other essential costs.

02

Logistics support

Proximity to MIA and FLL airports lets us source and ship food, medical supplies, and equipment to our partners even when local supply chains are disrupted.

03

Volunteer recruiting

We recruit and vet Haitian-American nurses, EMTs, and paramedics interested in supporting the mission, alongside students in nursing and pre-med programs.
